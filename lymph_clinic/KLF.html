What is Lymph Flow?

Lymphatic drainage therapy stimulates the lymphatic system, helping to remove toxins, reduce fluid retention, and boost your immune system. This therapy is particularly beneficial for individuals recovering from surgery, experiencing lymphedema, or simply seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.
