What is Lymph Flow?

Lymphatic drainage therapy stimulates the lymphatic system, helping to remove toxins, reduce fluid retention, and boost your immune system. This therapy is particularly beneficial for individuals recovering from surgery, experiencing lymphedema, or simply seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.

At Lymph Flow, our focus is on supporting your well-being through gentle and effective manual techniques. While our treatments can aid in relaxation and the promotion of a healthy lymphatic flow, they do not replace medical examinations or treatments by licensed healthcare professionals.

If your doctor recommends lymphatic drainage and issues a Verordnung (ärztliche Anordnung), you are welcome to bring it to us—we are happy to support your recovery within the scope of that referral.

Disclaimer: All services offered by Lymph Flow are wellness-based and non-medical in nature. They are intended to support the body’s natural functions and do not constitute medical advice or treatment. Clients are responsible for informing us of any health conditions, recent surgeries, or ongoing treatments before beginning therapy. We recommend consulting with a physician for any medical concerns.

Our Services

We offer a range of personalized lymphatic treatments tailored to your individual needs, including:

  • Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) – Gentle, rhythmic massage techniques to stimulate the lymphatic system, reduce swelling, and promote detoxification.

  • Post-Surgery Recovery Therapy – Specialized sessions to support healing, reduce fluid retention and bruising, and assist with tissue regeneration after cosmetic, orthopedic, or general surgeries.

  • Holistic Wellness Plans – Custom treatment plans that combine lymphatic therapy with lifestyle support for sustainable, long-term health benefits.

  • Lymphedema Support – Gentle, targeted care for managing primary or secondary lymphedema symptoms and improving comfort in coordination with medical guidance.

  • Relaxation and Stress Relief Sessions – Treatments that calm the nervous system, reduce stress, and enhance overall emotional and physical balance.
